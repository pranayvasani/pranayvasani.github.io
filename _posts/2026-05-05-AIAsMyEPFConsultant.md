# How AI Became My EPF Consultant and Actually Got Things Done

I had been putting off sorting my EPF accounts for last 2 to 3 years. Multiple jobs, multiple member IDs, and a lingering issue with my last employer's account that I never got around to fixing. The kind of financial admin that always feels too complicated to start. One afternoon I decided to just dump all my EPFO passbooks into a Claude conversation and see what happened.

What followed was surprisingly close to having a knowledgeable consultant sitting next to me.

Claude parsed through every passbook, mapped my entire employment history, identified the issue with my last employer's account I had never noticed. Within minutes I had a clear picture of my complete EPF portfolio, something I had never had despite being in the workforce for over 15 years.

But it didn't stop at analysis. Claude walked me through each action in real time, telling me exactly which portal to visit and what to do on each one:

---

### Bank KYC

on the UAN member portal (unifiedportal-mem.epfindia.gov.in)

My bank account had been showing a rejected KYC for last 3 years. Claude diagnosed the likely cause, guided me to delete the stale entry and re-initiate, and within hours it was green.

---

### Passbook Review

on the EPFO passbook portal (passbook.epfindia.gov.in)

Claude helped me read and interpret every transaction across all my accounts, connecting the dots across years of employment history in a way I had never done myself.

---

### Online Transfer Claim

back on the UAN member portal

Claude told me exactly which attestation option to select, what each step meant, and what to expect after submission.

---

### Grievance Filing

on the EPFO grievance portal (epfigms.gov.in)

When a transfer was blocked due to a detail mismatch, Claude drafted the exact grievance text, told me which category to select, and I had it filed in under 10 minutes. I want to specially thank the EPFO grievance team for their remarkably quick turnaround. Within days of filing, I received a clear resolution advising the next steps. For a government portal, that responsiveness genuinely impressed me.

---

### Joint Declaration

again on the UAN member portal

When EPFO advised a Joint Declaration to fix the mismatch, Claude immediately explained what it was and walked me through exactly where to find it and what to submit.

---

### HR Communication

Claude drafted a precise, professional message to my current employer's HR requesting approval for both the transfer claim and the Joint Declaration, with the right Member IDs, context, and a deadline callout.

---

Things that had been pending for years moved in a single afternoon.

The experience felt less like using a chatbot and more like having a consultant who had read every EPFO circular, remembered every account detail I had shared, and was available at 5 PM on a Sunday without billing by the hour.

AI won't replace the actual EPFO portal, your HR, or the processing time. But as a guide who helps you understand what to do, in what order, and exactly how to do it, it is genuinely transformative for navigating complex financial bureaucracy.

A special acknowledgment to the EPFO department for building and maintaining digital infrastructure that actually works. Between the UAN portal, the passbook portal, and the grievance portal, the entire process was online, trackable, and responsive. That makes a real difference for working professionals trying to manage their financial lives without taking days off to visit offices.

If you have unresolved EPF matters gathering dust, just start the conversation.
